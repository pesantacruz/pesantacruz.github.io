Welcome!

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at San Jose State University. My major areas of research are mobile and wireless networks, distributed algorithms, and communication theory.

During the Spring 2018 semester I will be teaching EE112: Introduction to Signal Processing and EE286: Mobile and Wireless Networking.
