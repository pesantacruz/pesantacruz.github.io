297A/B Projects

Students interested in working with me for their 297A/B projects must set up an appointment before the start of the semester. Students should prepare a small proposal with a project idea they would like to work on.

Groups for Master's projects must consist of 2 students. It is important to note that the project proposals must be aligned with the student's skills.

A full guideline to the process can be found here.

Advising

For advising, set up an appointment and make sure to have all the required forms filled up and ready when you come to the appointment. For Graduate advising, the required form can be found here.

I approve submitted Plans of Study once a week. If you have submitted a Plan of Study for me to approve, please send me an email to make sure I approve as soon as possible.
